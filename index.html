Recipe

  • 100g flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 stick butter
  • 100g sugar
  1. Sift flour into a bowl.
    1. Melt butter.
    2. Slowly beat butter and sugar together.
  2. Add the eggs one by one to butter and sugar mixture.
